D&D Backstories

Two-minute Teaser Demo

This piece of interactive fiction tells the story of how heros are made in the world of Dungeons and Dragons. All you need to play is a web browser and a love of adventure stories. If you're new to role-playing games, it will give you a feel for how they work. And if you're an experienced player, it will bring you back to basics and give you a chance to use those cantrips your dm is always poo-pooing.
