Backstories

This piece of interactive fiction tells the story of how heros are made in the world of Dungeons and Dragons. To play it, you only need a web browser and a love of adventure stories. If you're new to role-playing games that's totally fine, it will give you a feel for how they work. And if you're an experienced player, it will bring you back to basics and give you a chance to play with those cantrips your DM is always poo-pooing.

(No, I'm not associated with Wizards, so it is limited to just what's allowed under their Open Game License. But that's all I need to tell this story.)
